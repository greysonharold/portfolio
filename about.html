About Straight to the Point Archery:

Straight to the Point Archery is a non-profit education center founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen, an Olympic archer. We provide comprehensive archery education for all ages and skill levels, from beginners to professionals. Our facility boasts 28 shooting lanes, private coaching rooms, and access to outdoor shooting fields. We offer group lessons, private classes, birthday parties, corporate events, and tournaments. Our instructors are USA Archery-certified and provide expert guidance in a positive learning environment. We are dedicated to teaching valuable life skills through the discipline of archery.
